Momo

One of the favourite and comfort dishes of every Nepali. Momo are typically wrapped in dough and filled with minced meat such as chicken, buff, pork, or chopped vegetables. They are usually steamed or fried and served with chutney made of tomato and sesame seeds, which adds extra flavor.

Selroti

A traditional Nepali sweet with a crispy outer layer and soft inner texture, shaped like a ring. Made from rice flour, sugar, ghee, and water or milk, it is often paired with tea or curry and is a popular snack.

Samaybaji

Also known as the Newari khaja set, Samaybaji is a traditional Newar dish with over fifteen items like beaten rice, dried and fried meat, pickles, eggs, lentil pancakes, and bean curry. Often served with homemade drink aila and the dessert yomari.
