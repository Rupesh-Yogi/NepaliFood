The above pictures represent Nepali Cusine.So, Nepali food is a rich blend of flavors nfluenced by mainly Tibetan and then Chinese and Indian and maintain its different identity.It is primarily based on fresh, locally sourced ingredients and a balanced use of spices that enhance both taste and nutrition. The most common meal in Nepal is Dal Bhat, which consists of steamed rice (bhat) and lentil soup (dal), accompanied by vegetables, pickles, and sometimes meat or fish. This meal is nutritious and provides the necessary energy for daily life. "These dishes not only nourish the body but also reflect the cultural heritage and daily life of Nepal."
