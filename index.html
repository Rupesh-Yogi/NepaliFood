Dal Bhat - The Staple Dish

The above pictures represent Nepali cuisine. Nepali food is a rich blend of flavors influenced by Tibetan,
Chinese, and Indian cuisines. It maintains a unique identity, primarily based on fresh, locally sourced ingredients
and a balanced use of spices. One of the most common meals in Nepal is Dal Bhat, consisting of steamed rice
(bhat) and lentil soup (dal), accompanied by vegetables, pickles, and sometimes meat or fish. This meal is nutritious and provides necessary energy for daily life. "These dishes not only nourish the body but also reflect
the cultural heritage and daily life of Nepal."
